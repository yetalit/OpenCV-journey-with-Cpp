Picture Resolutions:
	VGA = 640 x 480 (pixels)
	HD = 1280 x 720
	FHD = 1920 x 1080
	4K = 3840 x 2160
	
Binary Image:
	A picture that contains only Black & White colors. So, each pixel has a value of '1' or '0' (2 Levels).

Gray Scale Image:
	A picture that contains 256 Levels (8 bit per pixel). We have a range of colors between Black{0} & White{255} (shades of gray).

RGB (Full Color) Images:
	These are composed of 3 Gray Scale Images representing the intensities of Red, Green and Blue.
	RGB VGA = 640 x 480 x 3
